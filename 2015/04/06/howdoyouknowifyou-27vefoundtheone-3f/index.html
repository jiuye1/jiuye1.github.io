How do you know if you've found “the one”?

发表于 2015-04-06   |   分类于 NONSENSE

如何确定生命中的“唯一”？

I LOVE Sasha Katsnelson’s answer.It makes my brain happy.But I’ll add this for the people who speak romance instead of logic.

我喜欢萨沙的回答，令我受益匪浅。但我想对因情废理之人多说几句。

This is advice I gave a younger person who said she knew shewanted to marry her boyfriend because she "loved him." Shethought that being "in love" meant she had found "the one."

这是我给一个年轻人的建议。这个年轻人说，她确定她想嫁给她的男朋友，因为她“爱他“。她认为”相爱“意味着她发现了生命中的”唯一“。

Are you a loving person? Because loving people are capable of loving lots of people in their lifetimes. You are likely tofind many people pleasant, agreeable, exciting, and enjoyable.That doesn’t mean you need to marry any of these people.

你是一个多情之人吗？多情会让你在一生中爱上很多人。你可能会遇到令你愉快的、兴奋的、激动的、甚至令你疯狂的人。但这并不意味着你都得嫁给他们。

At some point, you may find yourself loving a person whois particularly compatible with you. You find your ways ofthinking work well together. You have similar values in life.You have similar goals for your future.Congratulations! You have found a potential life-mate.not "the one" by any means, but a really good one.

某种程度上讲，你会特别容易爱上一个跟你合拍的人。你们的工作思路相通，价值观类似，未来规划都几乎相同。恭喜你，你找到了一个潜在的终身伴侣，不是”唯一“，但也蛮不错。

Does that person feel the same way? Do you both displayequal commitment to long-term relationships? Does your history together cover a wide range of experiences? Do you havea realistic idea of life together? now you have a decision tomake.

但那个人有同样的感觉吗？你们都对这段关系做出了长期许诺?你们共同经历了很多风风雨雨？你们有了共同面对现实生活的勇气？现在，你需要做一个决定。

Many people phrase their decision-making like this:

"This must be the right choice because I’ll never find anyone else as __ (good, special, wonderful) as this again."

oR

"This must be the right choice because I’ve never foundanyone else as __ (good, special, wonderful) as this before."

很多人这样表述：

”这一定是正确的决定，因为我再也找不到任何一个像他一样__(好、特别、完美）的人了。“

或者

”这一定是正确的决定，因为我从未遇到过任何一个像他一样__(好、特别、完美）的人。“

Statements like this are setting you up for disappointment! Instead, consider this:

•  You’re likely to find other people to love.

•  You’re likely to be compatible with some of them.

•  Some of them will possibly hold even greater potentialthan the person you are with now.

这样的表述令人失落。试想：

•  你可能会爱上别人。

•  你可能和其他一些人也合拍.

•  他们其中也许有更适合与你共度一生的人。

Ask this question instead: is your current relationshipgood enough that you are willing to take a pass on all future"potentially better" loves?

来回答这个问题：你们之间的爱，已经能够让你愿意为之放弃未来一切可能了吗？

Take some time to think about this. Rest on it, pray, meditate. Picture eating breakfast with that face (aging, sagging,wrinkling) across the table for the rest of your life.If the answer is still a solid "yes!" then go for it. But if youhave niggling doubts . . . give it more time。

好好想想这个问题。停下来，沉思、冥想。想象这样的场景：你余生的每一天早上，都将会和同一张脸共进早餐，而这张脸会慢慢衰老、松弛、下垂。如果你的回答依然是”Yes",那么就结婚吧！如果你依然犹豫，不妨再多给自己一些时间。
